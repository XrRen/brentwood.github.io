Scott P. Carnivale, MD, FACP
×

Dr. Scott Carnivale was born and raised in New Jersey. He received both his Bachelor's Degree and Medical Degree from Johns Hopkins University in Baltimore. Afterwards, he completed an Internal Medicine Residency at Johns Hopkins Hospital.


Dr. Carnivale joined Brentwood Medical Group in 1993 when he moved to Pittsburgh. He is a Fellow in the American College of Physicians.


Dr. Carnivale's interests include his family, golf, exercise and all of Pittsburgh's sports teams. He is an active member at his church as well as a Boy Scout leader. He is also an Eagle Scout.
