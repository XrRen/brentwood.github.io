Meet Our Team

Brentwood Medical's staff is here to serve you. We have a dedicated clinical and administrative crew to address your needs. Our front desk staff can assist you with making appointments, triaging messages to your provider, answering medical records questions and more. Our patient care coordinator can assist you in scheduling testing and obtaining pre-authorizations for those tests, as well as being able to answer any specific questions you may have on this care.

Our medical assistants are available to answer basic clinical questions, answering questions or taking requests for prescription refills, as well as obtaining vital signs, chief complaints and other pertinent information that the providers may need to give you the care you deserve and desire.
